Welcome to Sedat Airways


Sedat Airways was founded in 2021. The purpose of its establishment is to enable people to travel quickly and economically to all over the world. It has been carrying passengers proudly since the day it was founded. The company has always cared about the passengers and spent a lot of money on the safety of the passengers.
